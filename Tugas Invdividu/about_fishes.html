Dolphin
Click here

Dolphins are friendly sea creatures that live in the warmer side of the sea, ypically between 10 to 200m sea level. Dolphins have lungs despite being a sea creature and can hold their breath about seven or more minutes. Dolphins have a breathing hold on their head to breath.

Dolphins are very intelligent creatures that they're ranked 2nd in intelligence, just second to humans. That's crazy right?

Penguin
Click here

Penguin are animals that lives in oceans and coast. They tend to like the cold enviroment of the south and north pole. They're covered with black skin on each limbs and white skin on chest and belly. White and black skin have very interesting function, such as camouflage. When penguins swim, the white skin is on the bottom side, so predators thinks it's the sun. Same thing as the black skin, the black skin is on top of the penguin so birds cannot see them because it has the same color as the ocean. Cool right?
